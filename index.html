GetJarvis

View Live Demo

Jarvis is a web-app which lets any person to hire housekeeper/ home service professional/ cleaning jobs in Australia. The person can login/ signup to hire any worker. The Jarvis (housekeeper) looks after all requests each week, with payments and scheduling handles seamlessly in the background. Also, there are pages whereby any person can join an existing crew of workers. Each crew member has its own credentials. The housekeeper can find jobs nearby with the help of his location. Languages: Express.Js, Node.Js
